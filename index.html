Warhammer World

Introduction

Warhammer is a tabletop miniature wargame produced by Games Workshop. It's set in a fantasy or science fiction universe and involves detailed miniatures, intricate rules, and strategic gameplay.

History

The Warhammer universe has a rich history, with numerous editions and a vast array of lore. It has evolved over decades, influencing various other media, including video games, books, and more.

Factions

Warhammer features many factions, each with its own unique characteristics and units. Popular factions include the Space Marines, Eldar, Orks, and Tyranids.
